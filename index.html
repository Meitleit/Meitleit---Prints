About Meitleit

At Meitleit, we believe that printing should be effortless, efficient, and affordable for every business. As a trusted partner of world-renowned brands such as Ricoh, Fuji Xerox, and Fujifilm, we deliver advanced office printing and document management solutions tailored for companies of all sizes across Peninsular Malaysia.

Our services include printer rentals, sales, and maintenance, supported by responsive local teams who ensure your devices operate at peak performance. Whether you’re managing a small office or a large corporate network, Meitleit provides customized printing solutions that enhance productivity while keeping costs low.

We take pride in offering flexible rental packages starting from only RM60 per month, giving Malaysian businesses access to high-quality multifunction printers without heavy upfront investment. With a focus on reliability, transparency, and long-term customer satisfaction, Meitleit continues to grow as a trusted print solutions provider and corporate partner in the industry.
