How Meitleit works

Meitleit combines object mixing, acoustical modeling, and real-time rendering to produce an immersive soundstage mapped to your listening environment.

  • Object Mixer: Control individual audio objects independently.
  • Room Modeler: Simulate reflections & absorption for realism.
  • Renderer: High-performance binaural and multichannel output.
